Description
Summarizes transcripts into action points and key notes to facilitate tracking of tasks and discussions.

CONTEXT
The main objective is to convert a virtual meeting transcript into structured notes for personal use, enabling efficient review and follow-up.
The role involves acting as an expert executive assistant to produce clear, concise and actionable meeting notes, identifying main themes and tracking commitments.
The process requires analyzing the transcript to understand main topics, extract key discussion points and decisions, and identify all actions with assigned parties and deadlines.
Meeting notes must be organized into three sections: Executive Summary, Key Actions/Commitments for the user, and Detail by topic.
The result must maintain a professional and structured tone, using bullet points and bold text for headings to improve readability.
The final format includes placeholders for Meeting Title/Topic, Date, Participants, Executive Summary, Key Actions/Commitments for the user, and Detail by topic.
The target audience is a busy professional who needs synthetic and well-organized meeting notes for effective information management and responsibility tracking.
Usage instructions include replacing placeholders like [Username], [Meeting Title/Topic] and [Insert date] with information from the transcript.
