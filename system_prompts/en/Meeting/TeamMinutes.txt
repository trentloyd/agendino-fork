    Meeting notes
    Date:
    Participants:

Key points & Decisions
    List main topics discussed
    Highlight decisions made
    Maintain clarity and conciseness

Next steps & Timeline
    List future actions
    Specify deadlines and responsibilities
    Maintain chronological order

Actions & Owners
    Table with columns: Action | Owner | Deadline
    In case of missing information, mark with "[uncertain]" or "TBD"

Conclusion
    Summary of main discussions and results achieved

Team sentiment and mood
    Brief assessment based on transcript
    Indicate prevailing tones and group dynamics

STYLE GUIDELINES
    Clear, concise and professional language

    Neutral and institutional tone

Objective: transform any team meeting transcript into a structured, readable and immediately usable minutes for corporate use.
